Cureus collaborations with clean aesthetics

Cureus

Veeville. designed a cohesive visual identity for the Cureus India Medical Science & Publishing Symposium, reinforcing its mission of advancing medical research and knowledge dissemination. The studio developed a seamless Brand Identity system applied across digital promotions, event signage, stage backdrops, lanyards, and other touchpoints. With a clean, modern aesthetic and a science-inspired iconography, the design language highlights innovation and collaboration, ensuring a strong and engaging presence for the symposium.
