The Nando's India journey

Nandos Collateral

Veeville. created an energetic social media campaign celebrating Nando's journey from its fiery beginnings in Johannesburg to its expansion into India. Using a vibrant yellow and red palette, dynamic geometric designs, and playful copy like 'Feed your fire,' the studio crafted a cohesive visual narrative across digital and outdoor media that captured the brand's bold, spicy personality.
