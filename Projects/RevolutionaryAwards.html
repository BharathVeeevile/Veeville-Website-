RevolutioNari Awards

Veeville. crafted the brand identity for RevolutioNari, PepsiCo India's women empowerment initiative. The studio developed a cohesive visual system featuring the colors of the Indian flag and upward-moving arrows, bringing to life the program's mission across various touchpoints - from corporate materials to branded merchandise.
